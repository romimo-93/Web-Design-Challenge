Summary: Latitude vs. X

For this project I was asked to analyze weather data and compare it against latitude ranges to determine if specific weather types were affected by location on the globe. I used the Open Weather Map API and Google maps Geocoding to analyze weather data in over 500 cities, and then visualize the outcome using Matplotlib and Pandas. The different weather factors I analyzed against latitude were: humidity, cloudiness, maximum temperature, and wind speed. I found that the closer the latitude is to the equator the higher temperatures we will see, while further away from the equator wind-speeds will increase dramatically. Whereas, humidity and cloudiness seem to occur fairly regularly across all parts of the globe.
